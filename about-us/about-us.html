About Us

What we do at Orchidjobs Recruitment

Orchidjobs Recruitment provides bespoke recruitment services on an all-island basis to companies in all sectors and at every level
Orchidjobs Recruitment is part of a much larger organisation PRL, which provides a whole-market solution for many of Ireland’s most recognisable consumer brands – for more information visit our parent company website. PRL Recruitment is the no.1 sales, marketing & management recruitment consultancy operating on a whole island basis. Established in 1973 as Sales Placement Ltd., PRL Recruitment has remained the go-to recruiter for high calibre difficult to source talent for the island of Ireland.
We have a track record of placing professionals at all levels and we pride ourselves on matching
the talent in both skill set and cultural fit to an organisation.
